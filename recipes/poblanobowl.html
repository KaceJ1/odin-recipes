Poblano Bowl Recipe

Description

Delicious smokey poblano peppers with moist peppery turkey sitting on a bed of fluffy rice. Topped with the tang of lime zest.

This delicious meal is great for the whole family

Ingredients

  • 1 poblano
  • 1 turkey package
  • rice
  • roma tomato
  • two limes
  • sour cream
  • chili powder
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. start cooking rice
  2. cook turkey
  3. cook poblano
  4. zest lime and add to rice
  5. dice romat tomato
  6. combine sour cream, chili powder, salt, and pepper
  7. lay down rice on a plate, then add turkey and poblano on top
  8. add tomatos and drizzle on sour cream mixture
  9. squeeze half a lime on top
  10. enjoy!
